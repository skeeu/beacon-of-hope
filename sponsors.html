Our sponsors

This world extends far beyond the grasp of our hands.

The “Подари Жизнь” website is an online resource of a Russian charitable organization dedicated to fighting cancer in children. The site provides information on current projects and programs to support children with oncology, including treatment, rehabilitation and social assistance. Visitors can read the stories of the beneficiaries, participate in fundraising and become volunteers. The site also provides an opportunity to support the organization through donations and tells about the achievements and events related to the mission of Gift of Life.
Save the Children is a global charity focused on the rights and welfare of children around the world. The site provides information on current projects and programs aimed at ensuring the education, health and safety of children. Visitors can learn about donation and volunteer opportunities, as well as read success stories and news stories reflecting the organization's impact on children's lives. The site also offers resources including publications and research on child welfare. Up-to-date news, blogs and event information make the site a platform to actively engage and support Save the Children's mission.
The UNESCO website is a virtual platform for the United Nations organization dedicated to education, science, culture and related issues. The site provides information on projects and initiatives that support cultural heritage, education and scientific research around the world. Visitors can learn about global programs developed by UNESCO, including the protection of world heritage, media education, and support for scientific and cultural research. The site also provides resources and publications on current topics, as well as information on events and campaigns run by the organization. Overall, the UNESCO site is an important source of knowledge about global efforts in education, science and culture.
